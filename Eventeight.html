About this event

Yoga at the Lake IS BACK!
Join us for Yoga, Brunch & Mimosas on the HG Rooftop!

Start off the morning with an invigorating Yoga class at 10:00am, taught by CorePower Yoga. Followed by HG Sply Co Brunch & mimosas on the rooftop patio until 12:00pm!

What to bring: Yoga Mat - Towel - Water - ID


$30 ticket includes yoga, HG Brunch & mimosas until 12:00pm and a raffle ticket for a chance to win fun prizes! $20 ticket for Corepower All Access Members (must bring proof of membership)

Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to get settled! Class will begin at 10:00am.
Each month, CorePower Yoga will be featuring one of the class styles below. Check HG Sply Co social media for this month's class!
C2: Turn stress into sweat. This signature class strengthens, balances and detoxifies your entire body and mind as you move through more challenging postures and connected breath. Set to an energizing playlist, you’ll power up your yoga practice like never before.

Sculpt: When muscle meets yoga, Yoga Sculpt is born. Boost metabolism and build lean muscle mass as you move to upbeat tracks. You’ll combine body weight exercises with CorePower Yoga 2 sequencing and cardio to intensify each yoga pose while mixing in strength-training moves like squats, lunges and bicep curls.
